AIMetrical Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 8, 2025

Last Updated: September 8, 2025

AIMetrical is a Chrome extension designed to provide AI usage analytics and security protection while respecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices and your rights regarding any information that may be processed through normal extension operation.

Local Processing: The extension operates primarily on your device using local storage and processing. No cookies are used, and most functionality occurs locally without external data transmission.

Information We Collect

Usage Data (Anonymous)

During normal operation, AIMetrical may collect anonymous usage data to provide analytics and security services. This information cannot be used to identify you personally.

  • Platform Analytics: General usage patterns across supported AI platforms and related performance metrics.
  • Security Analytics: Information about security scanning activities and pattern detection results.
  • Technical Data: Basic technical information necessary for service operation and improvement.

Optional Information (With Your Consent)

You may choose to provide additional categorical information to access enhanced features. This is entirely optional.

  • Demographic Categories: General professional categories such as industry type, organization size, role function, and experience level.

Important: Optional data collection requires your explicit consent and can be declined without affecting core functionality.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We maintain strict data minimization practices. AIMetrical does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers: Names, addresses, contact information, or other identifying details.
  • Content data: AI conversations, prompts, responses, or any text content you interact with.
  • Sensitive information: Actual sensitive data content beyond pattern recognition.
  • Authentication data: Passwords, credentials, or login information.
  • General browsing: Web activity outside of intended AI platform functionality.
  • Location data: Geographic information or device location.

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Service Delivery: To provide analytics, insights, and security features as described in our product information.
  • User Experience: To deliver personalized recommendations and optimize extension performance.
  • Security Functions: To provide security scanning and protection features.
  • Product Development: To improve existing features and develop new functionality.

Research and Insights

We may use anonymous, aggregated information for research purposes, industry analysis, and service improvement. This research helps us better understand usage patterns and develop features that benefit our users and the broader community. All such analysis is conducted using aggregated data that cannot identify individual users.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell individual user data. We may share anonymous, aggregated information in certain circumstances:

  • For research and analysis purposes that benefit users and the community
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • For industry insights that help advance AI productivity and security practices
  • As required by law or legal process

Service Providers and Partners

We work with service providers for hosting, analytics, and other technical services. These providers are bound by appropriate data protection requirements and process information solely as needed to support our services.

Data Security and Retention

We implement security measures appropriate for the type of information processed. Much of our security analysis occurs locally on your device to minimize data transmission.

Data Minimization: We continuously review our data practices to ensure we only collect and retain information necessary for service functionality and user benefits.

  • Usage information: Retained as needed to provide analytics and service functionality.
  • Security data: Retained for the period necessary to provide security insights and improvements.
  • Optional information: Retained based on your consent preferences and can be removed upon request.
  • Local storage: Managed according to browser settings and extension configuration.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

  • Control: You can control optional data collection through extension settings.
  • Access: You can review available usage information through the extension interface.
  • Deletion: You can request information removal or uninstall the extension to remove associated data.
  • Consent Management: You can modify or withdraw consent for optional features at any time.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our services evolve or to reflect changes in data protection requirements. Significant updates will be communicated through appropriate channels, and continued use of the extension indicates acceptance of any changes.

Children's Privacy

AIMetrical is intended for professional and educational use and is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact Information

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection requirements including:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy and data protection laws
